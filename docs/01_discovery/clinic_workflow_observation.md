# DentApp — Clinic Workflow Observation Template

## 1. Purpose

This document is used to observe real dental practice workflows during the DentApp discovery phase.

The goal is to document what actually happens in the practice, not only what people say happens.

Observation should help identify:

- real workflow steps,
- responsibilities,
- information gaps,
- repeated delays,
- manual work,
- paper-based processes,
- memory-based processes,
- software opportunities,
- adoption risks.

No real patient personal data should be collected in this document.

Use anonymized descriptions only.

---

## 2. Observation Rules

During observation:

- Do not interrupt clinical work unnecessarily.
- Do not record real patient names.
- Do not record phone numbers, addresses, medical identifiers, or private details.
- Use anonymized labels such as Patient A, Patient B, Doctor 1, Assistant 1.
- Focus on workflow, not personal judgment.
- Record what happens, not what should happen.
- Note exceptions and workarounds.
- Pay attention to repeated small inefficiencies.
- Ask clarification questions after the workflow is complete, not during critical work.
