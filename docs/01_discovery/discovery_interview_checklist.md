# DentApp — Discovery Interview Checklist

## 1. Purpose

This document contains structured interview questions for the DentApp product discovery phase.

The goal is to understand how the pilot dental practice really works before implementing application features.

Interviews should focus on real workflows, problems, exceptions, responsibilities, and decisions.

Do not ask only what users want. Ask what they actually do.

---

## 2. General Interview Rules

During interviews:

- Ask for real examples.
- Ask what happens in normal cases.
- Ask what happens in exceptions.
- Ask who is responsible for each step.
- Ask where information is currently written down.
- Ask what is kept only in memory.
- Ask what causes delays, mistakes, or conflict.
- Avoid leading questions.
- Avoid suggesting software solutions too early.
- Do not collect real patient personal data.
- Use anonymized examples only.

---

## 3. Owner / Practice Manager Questions

### 3.1 Practice Structure

- How many doctors work in the practice?
- Are all doctors permanent or are some visiting specialists?
- How many assistants work in the practice?
- Is there a reception/admin person?
- Who manages scheduling?
- Who manages payments?
- Who manages materials?
- Who makes final business decisions?

### 3.2 Main Problems

- What are the biggest daily operational problems?
- Where is the most time lost?
- Which information is hardest to find?
- Which mistakes happen repeatedly?
- What causes conflict between staff members?
- What currently depends too much on memory?

### 3.3 Financial Control

- How are services priced?
- Is there one price list or multiple price lists?
- How are discounts handled?
- How are unpaid balances tracked?
- How are installment payments tracked?
- How are advances tracked?
- Who is allowed to see financial information?
- What financial reports would be useful?

### 3.4 Doctor Commission

- Do doctors work on percentage?
- Is the percentage the same for all doctors?
- Does percentage depend on service type?
- Is commission calculated when work is performed or when payment is collected?
- How are laboratory costs handled?
- How are visiting specialists paid?
- How often are doctors paid?
- What report would each doctor need?
- What report would the owner need?

### 3.5 Inventory and Materials

- Who tracks materials?
- Who notices that something is missing?
- Who orders materials?
- Who approves purchases?
- How are urgent shortages handled?
- Which materials are most problematic?
- Are expiry dates tracked?
- Are batch/lot numbers important?
- What reports would help with inventory?

### 3.6 Reporting

- What should the owner see daily?
- What should the owner see weekly?
- What should the owner see monthly?
- Which reports are currently missing?
- Which numbers are currently estimated manually?
- What would make the practice easier to manage?

---

## 4. Doctor Questions

### 4.1 Patient Record Workflow

- How do you currently review a patient before treatment?
- Where do you check previous interventions?
- Where do you write clinical notes?
- What information do you need immediately when opening a patient record?
- What information is usually missing?
- How do you track allergies, warnings, or important medical notes?
- Do you use paper records, digital records, or both?

### 4.2 Examination and Diagnosis

- What happens during the first examination?
- What information is recorded?
- How is odontogram status recorded?
- How are diagnoses written?
- How are photos or X-rays linked to the patient?
- What is difficult to find later?

### 4.3 Treatment Plan

- How do you create a treatment plan?
- Is the plan written down or mostly verbal?
- Do patients receive a written plan?
- How are treatment options presented?
- How are accepted and rejected treatments tracked?
- How are changes to the plan tracked?
- How do you know what should happen at the next appointment?
- What is the biggest problem with current treatment planning?

### 4.4 Performed Services

- How do you record what was performed?
- When do you record it?
- Who records it?
- How is the performed service linked to price?
- How are unfinished procedures tracked?
- How are complications or notes recorded?

### 4.5 Payments and Debt Awareness

- Do doctors need to know if the patient owes money?
- Do doctors discuss payment with patients?
- How do you know if something from previous work is unpaid?
- What financial information should be visible to doctors?
- What financial information should not be visible?

### 4.6 Doctor Commission

- Do you want to see your own performed work?
- Do you want to see collected vs uncollected amounts?
- Do you want to see expected commission?
- How should corrections be handled?
- What would make commission calculation more transparent?

---

## 5. Specialist / Visiting Doctor Questions

- How often do you work in the practice?
- How do you receive patient information before arrival?
- What information do you need before treatment?
- Do you need access to full patient history or only assigned cases?
- How do you record performed work?
- How is your payment/commission calculated?
- What report would help you verify your work and payment?

---

## 6. Assistant Questions

### 6.1 Daily Work

- What do you check at the beginning of the day?
- How do you prepare for scheduled patients?
- How do you know which materials are needed?
- How do you help during treatment?
- What information do you write down?
- What causes delays during the day?

### 6.2 Patient Support

- Do you help with patient records?
- Do you help with consent forms?
- Do you prepare documentation?
- Do you help with treatment plan communication?
- What information do patients most often ask for?

### 6.3 Materials

- Which materials do you use most often?
- How do you know when something is low?
- How do you report missing material?
- Who do you tell?
- What happens if nobody orders it?
- What would make material tracking easier?

---

## 7. Reception / Administration Questions

### 7.1 Scheduling

- How are appointments currently scheduled?
- Is Google Calendar used?
- Who creates appointments?
- Who changes appointments?
- How are cancellations handled?
- How are no-shows handled?
- How is appointment duration estimated?
- How are doctors and chairs coordinated?
- What appointment information must be visible immediately?

### 7.2 Patient Communication

- How do patients contact the practice?
- How are phone calls recorded?
- Are reminders sent manually?
- Are confirmations tracked?
- What information is needed when a patient calls?
- What are the most common patient questions?

### 7.3 Payments

- Who records payments?
- How are partial payments recorded?
- How are unpaid balances checked?
- How are patients reminded about debt?
- What happens when a patient says they will pay next time?
- What payment report is needed at the end of the day?

### 7.4 Documents

- Which documents are printed?
- Which documents are signed?
- Which documents are scanned?
- Where are documents stored?
- Which documents are hard to find later?

---

## 8. Inventory Responsible Person Questions

### 8.1 Current Inventory Process

- Is there a current material list?
- Is inventory tracked in a notebook, Excel, software, or memory?
- Who updates stock levels?
- How often is inventory checked?
- Which materials are critical?
- Which materials frequently run out?

### 8.2 Requests and Ordering

- How does someone request material?
- Is there an approval step?
- Who decides what to buy?
- Who contacts suppliers?
- Who receives delivered materials?
- How are received materials recorded?
- Are purchase prices tracked?

### 8.3 Stock Data

- Should minimum stock be defined?
- Should expiry dates be tracked?
- Should batch/lot numbers be tracked?
- Should location be tracked?
- Should material consumption be linked to procedures?
- Which reports would be useful?

---

## 9. Workflow-Specific Questions

### 9.1 New Patient Workflow

- How does a new patient usually contact the practice?
- What information is collected first?
- When is the patient record created?
- Is anamnesis completed before or during the visit?
- Who enters the information?
- What happens during the first examination?
- Is a treatment plan always created?
- Is price discussed immediately?
- Is the next appointment scheduled immediately?

### 9.2 Existing Patient Workflow

- How is the patient found in current records?
- How is previous treatment reviewed?
- How is the next step identified?
- What happens if the doctor who treated the patient is not present?
- What information is usually missing?
- How are old plans continued or changed?

### 9.3 End-of-Day Workflow

- Is there an end-of-day review?
- Who checks payments?
- Who checks performed services?
- Who checks doctor work?
- Who checks materials?
- What report would be useful at the end of the day?

---

## 10. Closing Questions

Ask every interviewee:

- If DentApp could solve only one problem for you, what should it solve?
- Which screen would you open most often?
- What would make you stop using the application?
- What information must be available in one click?
- What current process should not be changed?
- What should definitely be improved?
- What should the application never make more complicated?
